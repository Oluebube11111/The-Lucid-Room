Lizzo Speaks Out on Public Commentary & Unrealistic Beauty Standards

Published Recently · By Editorial Team

In a recent interview, Lizzo candidly addressed the ongoing public focus regarding her body, stating:

“I’ve gained 20 pounds since last year, so everyone can ask me about weight gain, too, if they’d like.”

She went on to criticize what she described as a broader culture that constantly judges women’s bodies, arguing that the pressure to meet unrealistic beauty standards affects how women are viewed and treated.
